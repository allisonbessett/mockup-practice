Pricing

At Kitties To Go, you can rent a kitty by the hour or for the day!

Required for Cat Bookings

In order to check out a cat, we require you to fill out paperwork(first time only!), have a litter box, and have a cat-friendly space. Please do not let the cats outside. If you have a change of address, phone number, ect., please let us know.
